# Token System

PowerAds uses a token-based billing system. Every video generation consumes tokens, which are deducted from your account balance as each step of the pipeline runs.

## How Tokens Work

Tokens are deducted **per pipeline step** as your video progresses through generation:

| Step | Description | Token Cost |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Script generation | AI writes the ad script | Low |
| Text-to-speech | Voiceover generation per scene | Low |
| Image generation | AI-generated visuals per scene | Medium |
| Video generation | Video clip rendering per scene | High |
| Stitching | Final assembly and encoding | Low |

The total cost depends on:
- **Video tier** — Premium costs more than Basic
- **Number of scenes** — More scenes = more TTS, image, and video steps
- **Aspect ratio** — Wider formats may use slightly more compute

## Video Tiers

| Tier | Quality | Speed | Token Cost |
|------|---------|-------|------------|
| `basic` | Good — suitable for drafts and testing | Faster | Lower |
| `premium` | Best — production-ready quality | Standard | Higher |

Your token balance is visible in the **Dashboard → Billing** section.

## Insufficient Tokens

If your balance is too low to start a video, the API returns:

<CodeBlock
  language="json"
  title="402 Payment Required"
  code={`{
  "error": "Insufficient tokens. Please top up your balance.",
  "required": 150,
  "available": 20
}`}
/>

<Callout type="error" title="Tokens are non-refundable per step">
  Tokens are deducted as each pipeline step completes. If a job fails midway, tokens for completed steps are still consumed. Failed steps themselves are not charged.
</Callout>

## Buying Tokens

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Choose a token package
3. Pay via credit card
4. Tokens are credited immediately

## Best Practices

- **Start with Basic tier** for testing and drafts, then switch to Premium for final production
- **Monitor your balance** before batch-generating multiple videos
- **Use webhooks** to track completion and avoid wasting tokens on redundant status polling
- **Provide detailed inputs** — better descriptions lead to better first-try results, reducing the need to regenerate
